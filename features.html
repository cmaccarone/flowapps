Initial Interview

In this step we will do a simple call and go over the project's requirements and potential solutions. All requirements are documented and recored in our agile project management system (jira) so we dont miss anything. After we are finished we will meet with our development team and get back to you with a project cost estimate.

Wireframes

After we have agreed an estimated price and a contract has been signed we will proceed with the design process. The first step in the design process is wireframing, which is just a simple quick layout of the app. This first step gives us a good idea of where buttons will be and what the user's experience will be using the core features of the app. This low fidelity design saves us time in the long run and keeps us from having to redesign things later.

High Fidelity Designs

Our goal here is to get the designs pixel perfect for you. From colors to logo and animations we want the designs to look as close to the finished app as we can.

Engineering Phase

Once designs are done or complete/stable enough to begin we start the engineering process. Sometimes we start this process right away even before the designs have been completed. We can do this for some features like onboarding. We get the features working and then implement the designs later so that our engineering team doesn't have to wait during the lag time of designs getting finished.

Testing and Production

As we are building the app we will constantly be releasing new versions of it so you can test it as we work. Once we are done with testing and implemenging all your features we release the app on the respective app stores.
