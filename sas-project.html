The sportaircraftseats.com Story

Oct 30 2021 • By Caleb Maccarone

When you are managing a medium sized business sometimes its easy to loose sight of the details. It's easy to just get stuck in the day to day and not have the time to optimize and grow. Custom software helps solve this problem.

Problem

When Sport Aircraft Seats first contacted us they where just using a simple whiteboard system to track their orders which worked well, but to be effective the whiteboard also required its own system. As the company began to scale the whiteboard filled up and the opperational overhead of keeping it up to date created a need for a more scalable solution.

Defining Core Features

The first step in building a solution was analyzing the current system and finding ways to optimize it. After some research we decided that the core features would be:

  • Automated order fetching from the website
  • Manufacturing process tracking
  • Customer detail / order tracking
  • Employee Management
  • Employee Job Delegation

Design

Wireframes

After the core features have been defined we started the design process. This process begins by wireframing the entire app. Wireframes are just low fidelity "Stick Figure" like designs that determine where the buttons will be and how the core features will be laid out this normally only takes a few days.

When wireframing was completed we started on the high fidelity designs. This process can take anywhere from three weeks to two months depending on the project.
